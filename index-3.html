Staff Profile

Timothous L. Shannon

Timothous received his national certification in therapeutic massage & bodywork from Heritage College, graduating from a 1,450 program in 2008. He is registered and licensed as a therapist in California, Colorado, Nevada and Utah and is insured through Associated Bodywork & Massage Professionals (ABMP) as a Certified Member.

In an effort to better serve his clients, Timothous began his post-graduate studies in Oriental Medicine at Southwest Acupuncture College in Boulder Colorado and later attended Yo San University of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Acupuncture in Los Angeles, California.

Timothous is passionate about educating his patients and empowering them to take control of their own health and well-being. He has a mindful, nurturing touch and a relaxed, professional presence that allows him to work well with a variety of individuals. He has experience working with hundreds of clients who have recent as well as old injuries, injuries due to overuse, weekend warriors, cancer survivors, joint replacements, clients who have diabetes, fibromyalgia, need pain management as well as pre and post natal. He is predominantly focused on an integrated, holistic approach to healing that addresses the root causes of the injury or illness.

Timothous is also the Founder & Head Shihan of Usui Reiki International, one of the few organizations in the United States dedicated to teaching traditional Japanese Reiki.
